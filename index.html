A brief history of cars

  1. Early Beginnings (Late 1700s–1800s):

    2. The idea of a self-propelled vehicle dates back to 1769, when Nicolas-Joseph Cugnot built the first steam-powered vehicle.1807: François Isaac de Rivaz designed an internal combustion engine powered by hydrogen gas.1886: Karl Benz patented the first practical automobile powered by an internal combustion engine. This is considered the birth year of the modern car.

    Modern Innovations

    • Hybrid Systems: Combine gas engines and electric motors for better fuel efficiency.
    • Autonomous Driving: Uses sensors, cameras, and AI for self-driving capabilities.
    • Electric Vehicles (EVs): Run entirely on batteries (e.g., Tesla, Rivian).
    • Connectivity: Smart features like navigation, remote control via apps, and in-car Wi-Fi.
